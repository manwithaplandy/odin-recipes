Roasted Lemon Herb Salmon

Ingredients:

  • Salmon Filet (Approx 12oz)
  • 3 medium-sized lemons
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Extra-Virgin Olive Oil (approx 4 tbs)
  • Oregano (dried or fresh)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees, with shelf on the middle rack
  2. In glass baking dish, cover bottom with thin layer of olive oil
  3. Pour some oil on top of the filet and spread to cover the whole filet evenly
  4. Halve one of the lemons and squeeze both halves over the whole filet
  5. Season filet with salt, pepper, and oregano
  6. Slice remaining lemon into 1/4" thick slices, and lay slices on top of filet, discarding heels
  7. Place baking dish on the middle rack in the oven and bake for 13-15 minutes
  8. Salmon is done when it reaches an internal temperature of 145 degrees
