All-American Hamburger

Ingredients:

  • Ground Beef, 1lb
  • Romaine Lettuce
  • Beefsteak tomato
  • Red onion
  • Brioche Hamburger buns
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Worsteshire sauce
  • Ketchup
  • Spicy Brown Mustard

Instructions:

  1. Pre-heat grill on medium-high heat
  2. Slice tomato into 1/4" thick slices
  3. Tear lettuce into round pieces 3-4" across
  4. Slice onion into thin slices
  5. Add 2tsp of Worsteshire sauce to ground beef, and season well with Salt & Pepper
  6. Form ground beef into 4 patties about 1/2" thick
  7. Place ground beef patties in the middle of the grill, do not press down on patties
  8. Grill for about 3-4 minutes per side, flipping minimally, until internal temperature reaches 165F
  9. When the burgers are almost done, place the buns on the grill to toast for 2-3 minutes
  10. Spread spicy brown mustard on buttom bun, top with burger patty
  11. Stack lettuce, tomato, and onion on top of the patty
  12. Spread ketchup on the top bun and place bun on top of the rest of the burger
  13. Serve with an ice cold beer
